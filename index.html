THE EFFECTS OF MOBILE GAMES TO THE ACADEMIC PERFORMANCE

Introduction

Even before the pandemic started, there was a lot of gamers and people interested in mobile games. Mobile games have both positive and negative effects on students. Mobile games are one of the factors affecting students' academic performance. Because of mobile games, some students don't prioritize their academics and studies. Playing mobile games is one of students' habits or regular routine.

The problem is that many students have become dependent on playing mobile games, which makes it difficult for them to ma3nage their time, especially when studying. Playing mobile games can result in depressive disorders, anxiety, and aggressive attitudes and actions. Theshort attention spans are destroyed by mobile gaming.

On this website,we'd like to know what percentage of students are interested in or enjoy mobile gaming. It also providepositive and negative effects based on the responses of SP- ICT Grade 10 students. It also aims to assess and find out the impact or effects of mobile-gaming to the academic performance of the students.

This website will assist students in breaking their addiction to playing mobile games. Not just the students, but also their teachers and parents, should be aware of how mobile games affect students' academic performance
